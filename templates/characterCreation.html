Character Creation

Class Selection

Warrior

  • Main Attribute: Strength

  • Max Armor Reduction: 60%

  • Health Multiplier: 3.5x

  • Can only equip shields in your off-hand

  • The warrior has a 25% chance to block an attack when wearing a shield

Berserker

  • Main Attribute: Strength

  • Max Armor Reduction: 45%

  • Health Multiplier: 2.8x

  • Takes 2x damage from magic

  • The berserker has a 50% chance to attack again after each hit

Magic Knight

  • Main Attribute: Strength

  • Max Armor Reduction: 50%

  • Health Multiplier: 3x

  • Only uses one (one-handed) weapon

  • For every successful hit, drop the foes armor reduction by 5%

Fencer

  • Main Attribute: Strength

  • Max Armor Reduction: 40%

  • Health Multiplier: 2.7x

  • Only uses one (one-handed) weapon

  • Has an additional 25% critical chance (max is increased to 60%). Drops the armor reduction of foes by 15%

Frost Mage

  • Main Attribute: Intelligence

  • Max Armor Reduction: 20%

  • Health Multiplier: 2x

  • Your magic attacks cannot be blocked or dodged

  • A tankier mage. Their icy aura drops the critical chance of foes by 20%

Fire Mage

  • Main Attribute: Intelligence

  • Max Armor Reduction: 15%

  • Health Multiplier: 1.7x

  • Your magic attacks cannot be blocked or dodged

  • Every attack applies a burn, which increases 15% of base damage each hit

Dark Mage

  • Main Attribute: Intelligence

  • Max Armor Reduction: 15%

  • Health Multiplier: 1.9x

  • Your magic attacks cannot be blocked or dodged

  • On each hit, you have a 50% chance to steal a third of your base damage as health

Blood Mage

  • Main Attribute: Intelligence

  • Max Armor Reduction: 15%

  • Health Multiplier: 1.8x

  • Your magic attacks cannot be blocked or dodged

  • Instead of dealing damage, critical hits heal you for half of the base damage. If the foe is a mage, healing is doubled

Scout

  • Main Attribute: Dexterity

  • Max Armor Reduction: 30%

  • Health Multiplier: 2.3x

  • Reflexes: You have a 25% chance to evade physical attacks

  • Has a 15% chance to ignore Warriors blocks and a 15% chance to evade magical attacks

Rogue

  • Main Attribute: Dexterity

  • Max Armor Reduction: 20%

  • Health Multiplier: 1.8x

  • Reflexes: You have a 25% chance to evade physical attacks

  • Strikes twice on each turn

Demon Hunter

  • Main Attribute: Dexterity

  • Max Armor Reduction: 35%

  • Health Multiplier: 2.3x

  • Wears warrior equipment but uses ranged weapons. Has a 35% chance of restoring all hitpoints when killed

Assassin

  • Main Attribute: Dexterity

  • Max Armor Reduction: 20%

  • Health Multiplier: 1.8x

  • Reflexes: You have a 25% chance to evade physical attacks

  • Has an additional 20% critical chance (max is increased to 50%). Your critical hits deal 3x damage instead of 2x
