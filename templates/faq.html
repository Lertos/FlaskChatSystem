General Info

Drop Rates

  • Under Level 5 - 70%
  • Over Level 5 - 45%
  • Bounties - 75%
  • Dungeons - 100%
  • Drop Chance Blessing - +20%

The Arena

  • Win - Lower Honor Than Opponent: +8 Honor
  • Win - Higher Honor Than Opponent: +6 Honor
  • Loss - Lower Honor Than Opponent: -8 Honor
  • Loss - Higher Honor Than Opponent: -10 Honor

Monsters

  • Dungeons - These are fights that are pre-defined and are the same for everyone
  • Quests/Bounties - These fights scale based on your current stats at the point of combat

Icon Meanings

  • Gold - Used to upgrade your attributes on the dashboard
  • Experience - Experience is what determines your level
  • Stamina - Allows you to start quests and bounties
  • Travel Time - How long quests/bounties will take
  • Honor - Earned (and lost) in the arena fighting other players
  • Loot - Lets the player know they received loot
  • Dungeon Key - How many times you may attempt fighting dungeons
  • Bounty Attempts - How many bounties you are allowed to attempt for the day
  • Arena Attempts - How many players you can fight in the arena for the day

Classes

  • Warrior

    • Main Attribute: Strength
    • Max Armor Reduction: 60%
    • Health Multiplier: 3.5x
    • Weapons: One Handed
    • Special 1: Can equip shields in your off-hand
    • Special 2: Has a 25% chance to block an attack

  • Berserker

    • Main Attribute: Strength
    • Max Armor Reduction: 45%
    • Health Multiplier: 2.8x
    • Weapons: One Handed
    • Special 1: Takes 2x damage from mages
    • Special 2: Has a 50% chance to attack again after each hit

  • Magic Knight

    • Main Attribute: Strength
    • Max Armor Reduction: 50%
    • Health Multiplier: 3.0x
    • Weapons: One Handed
    • Special 1: For every hit, drop the foes armor reduction by 5%

  • Fencer

    • Main Attribute: Strength
    • Max Armor Reduction: 40%
    • Health Multiplier: 2.7x
    • Weapons: One Handed
    • Special 1: Has an additional 25% critical chance (max is now 60%)
    • Special 2: Drops the foes armor reduction by 15%

  • Scout

    • Main Attribute: Dexterity
    • Max Armor Reduction: 30%
    • Health Multiplier: 2.3x
    • Weapons: Two Handed
    • Special 1: Has a 25% chance to evade physical attacks
    • Special 2: Has a 15% chance to evade magic attacks/ignore blocking
    • Special 3: Has a 15% chance to ignore Warrior's blocks

  • Rogue

    • Main Attribute: Dexterity
    • Max Armor Reduction: 20%
    • Health Multiplier: 1.8x
    • Weapons: One Handed
    • Special 1: Has a 25% chance to evade physical attacks
    • Special 2: Strikes twice on each turn

  • Demon Hunter

    • Main Attribute: Dexterity
    • Max Armor Reduction: 35%
    • Health Multiplier: 2.3x
    • Weapons: Two Handed
    • Special 1: Has a 35% chance to resurrect with full health
    • Special 2: A ranged-tank hybrid

  • Assassin

    • Main Attribute: Dexterity
    • Max Armor Reduction: 20%
    • Health Multiplier: 1.8x
    • Weapons: One Handed
    • Special 1: Has a 25% chance to evade physical attacks
    • Special 2: Has an additional 20% critical chance (max is now 50%)
    • Special 3: Your critical hits deal 3x damage instead of 2x

  • Frost Mage

    • Main Attribute: Intelligence
    • Max Armor Reduction: 20%
    • Health Multiplier: 2.0x
    • Weapons: Two Handed
    • Special 1: Your magic attacks cannot be blocked or dodged
    • Special 2: Drops your foes critical chance by 20%

  • Fire Mage

    • Main Attribute: Intelligence
    • Max Armor Reduction: 15%
    • Health Multiplier: 1.7x
    • Weapons: Two Handed
    • Special 1: Your magic attacks cannot be blocked or dodged
    • Special 2: Increases damage by 15% each turn

  • Dark Mage

    • Main Attribute: Intelligence
    • Max Armor Reduction: 15%
    • Health Multiplier: 1.9x
    • Weapons: Two Handed
    • Special 1: Your magic attacks cannot be blocked or dodged
    • Special 2: Has a 50% chance to steal 1/3 of your base damage as health

  • Blood Mage

    • Main Attribute: Intelligence
    • Max Armor Reduction: 15%
    • Health Multiplier: 1.8x
    • Weapons: Two Handed
    • Special 1: Your magic attacks cannot be blocked or dodged
    • Special 2: Critical hits heal you for 1/2 of your base damage instead of doing damage
    • Special 3: The healing is twice as strong if the foe is a mage
